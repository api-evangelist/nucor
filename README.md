# Nucor (nucor)
Nucor Corporation is a Fortune 500 American steel and steel products manufacturer headquartered in Charlotte, North Carolina. It is the largest steel producer in the United States and the largest recycler of any material in North America. This repository captures the public corporate, sustainability, and certification resources for Nucor.
